Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At TibetanKeys, we are committed to digital literacy and the absolute privacy of our users. Because we value your trust, we have built this platform to be as private as possible.

1. Zero Data Collection

We do not require users to create accounts, log in, or provide personal information to use our typing lessons or the "Page" word processor. We do not track your personal identity or browsing habits.

2. Editor Privacy (The "Page" Tool)

All typing performed within the TibetanKeys Page editor happens locally on your device. We do not store, see, or transmit the text you type. When you click "Save as Word," the file is generated directly in your browser and saved to your computer; no copy is kept on our servers.

3. Voluntary Contributions

When you support us via UPI QR Code or PayPal, your transaction is handled directly by your chosen banking app or PayPal. TibetanKeys does not receive or store your bank account numbers, credit card details, or financial passwords.

4. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external resources (such as Monlam IT for software downloads). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites.
